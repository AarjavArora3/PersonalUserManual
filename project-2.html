Expectations

  • I am writing this because...I hope to overcome the ambiguity associated with creating a picture about a person/ potential employee from a piece of paper. In short, humans are complex and this is just another way to bridge that gap.

  • What result I hope by writing and sharing this...
    1. Finding commonalities such as goals, aspirations and values
    2. Method of aligning our working styles and personalities according to the common goal(s)
    3. Creating a fulfilling connection
  • How I do not hope this manual is perceived...
    1. This manual is a way to shorten the learning curve when we create new teams or start working with new people.
    2. I follow and appreciate an open-minded approach when interacting & working with others. This manual is supposed to be a headstart to following that approach.
