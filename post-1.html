A glimpse into communication with me

  • Acknowledgement that you don’t appreciate me cutting or correcting or interrupting you, etc. I might get a bit too excited and engrossed in the task at hand and think out loud, which might not align with everyone’s way of working or communicating or receiving ideas/ criticism of their ideas.
  • Collaborative — I get pig headed when people tell me what to do, or arrogantly assert they know best (although I realise the people that do this probably don’t realise it themselves…!)
  • High context communication — Me vs. Words - An ever-evolving battle. High context communication is commonplace with me. I don't expect you to be on the lookout for intricate signs in my behavior, but hope that you will understand that I always have a positive intent.
  • Give me time and space, I like to think things through.
