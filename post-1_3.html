Professional Growth Areas

My Professional Growth Areas are:

  • Note taking is not my forte. This affects the way I and my co-workers perceive and approach a learning or problem. Trying to be better with written communication to prevent loss of important information and communication.
  • Over-analyzing has been one of my weaknesses that doesn’t work well in situations needing tangible and practical thinking.

    • Personal Growth Areas

    My Personal Growth Areas are:

  • Honesty (bare with me): backfires more than expected. In corporate settings, it requires us to create a balance between sensemaking and self-integrity.
  • My direct approach can be perceived as rude or blunt. I am trying to work on it and you might not even see it as a problem at the time we are working together (fingers crossed). #PardonMyLanguage.
  • Time and space to process thoughts: I need time to process thoughts, same applies to my values that could potentially affect my co-workers. Empathy, for example, is one that I possess, but might not show in the first expression or even later on (as it becomes “too late” to express).
  • My way of tackling Emotional Intelligence situations has been to stay behind my introversion and individualist nature to try and deal with it on my own and not bother others. I would likely keep my emotions to myself unless approached.
